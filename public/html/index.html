Software Engineering Research Lab - IIITA

The SERL at IIIT Allahabad will conduct research on designing scalable solutions for complex software systems driven by business needs. It aims to cover cross-disciplinary research domains and address industrial software engineering challenges. Collaborating with industry experts is necessary to evaluate and provide effective solutions to challenges in data-driven industries, cyber physical systems, smart city applications, and business intelligence.
